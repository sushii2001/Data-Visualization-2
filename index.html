Instagram users all around the World

Instagram, the world's ranked 4th social media platform, which contains over 1.386 billion number of users. Where the gender distribution splits into 48.6% males and 51.4% females. Besides that, the country having the most number of users is India. However, this is due to the high population density, where as the true concentrated number of users is in United States after normalising.
Moreover, the fastest growth rate of Instagram has over 100 million in just 9 months. However, observing the bar graph, it is still far from being on par with Facebook and Youtube.

The word cloud beside is a collection of the most used hashtags in Instagram among the users. For example, #love is used around a total of 2 billion times. As hashtaging is a norm thing to do in Instagram to attract other users and connect with the world by either posting feeds, stories or reels.
These are the content that are found in Instagram and hashtaging allows the common interest users to stay connected and create a larger community through mutual interest in a social media platform.
Apart from that, the average time spent on Instagram is 29 minutes daily.

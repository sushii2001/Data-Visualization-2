Instagram users all around the World

Instagram, the world ranked 4th social media platform, having over 1.386 billion number of users. Where most of the users are from India. However, due to high population density and after normalising it, the true concentrated number of users can be observed is from US. There was a report back in 2020, which reported 9.5% of the users in Instagram are fake bots.

Besides, the gender distribution splits into 48.6% males and 51.4% females. Moreover, the growth rate of Instagram has reached over 100 million in just 9 months. Although it is still far from being on par with Facebook and Youtube (according to the bar graph), with this rapid growth rate, it is not surprising that one day, Instragram will also reach the top of the social media platform.

The word cloud beside is a collection of the most used hashtags in Instagram among the users. As hashtaging is a norm thing to do in Instagram to attract other users and connect with the world by either posting feed, story or reels (short video clips). These are the content that are found in Instagram and hashtaging allows the common interest users to stay connected and create a larger community through mutual interest in a social media platform. Apart from that, The average time spent on Instagram, is around 29 minutes a day.
